Strawberry Fluff

Description

This is a favorite holiday treat for my family. It can be made with sugar free gelatin, fat free cottage cheese, and fat free cool whip for a lower calorie treat. This is an EASY and quick treat!

Ingredients

  • 2 (3 ounce) packages strawberry flavored Jell-OÂ®
  • 1 (16 ounce) package cottage cheese
  • 1 (8 ounce) container frozen whipped topping, thawed
  • 1 (20 ounce) can crushed pineapple, drained

Procedure

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine the gelatin mix, cottage cheese, whipped topping and pineapple.
  2. Mix together well, refrigerate until chilled and serve.
  3. Enjoy!
